    Personiskā Ekspozija

    Project overview: I always had a living desire to be free, and the dynamic nature of Venice Beach is what attracted me to this area. Two years later I have found my place between skateboarders, fortune tellers, hippie beach bums and surfer bros. This is the photographic story about the spirit of Venice Beach.

    Details: Documentary/exposé book, includes information I have written, photographed and collected.
